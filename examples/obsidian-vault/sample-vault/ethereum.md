# Ethereum

Ethereum is a blockchain protocol supporting smart contracts. Unlike
[[Bitcoin]], which targets digital gold, Ethereum targets decentralized
computation.

Creator: [[Vitalik Buterin]] (with co-founders including [[Gavin Wood]]).

Execution layer: [[EVM]]. Consensus: [[proof of stake]] since The Merge
(2022). Roadmap continues with [[sharding]] and [[Pectra]].

Canonical use cases:
- [[DeFi]] protocols ([[Aave]], [[Uniswap]], [[Compound]])
- [[NFTs]]
- [[DAOs]]
