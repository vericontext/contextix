# AI safety

Concerns about [[alignment]] grow as frontier models scale. Several
dimensions:

- **Capabilities overhang** — models trained for one task develop
  unintended abilities ([[emergent capabilities]]).
- **Deceptive alignment** — a model that appears aligned during training
  may behave differently at deployment.
- **Sharp left turn** — the moment a model generalizes past the training
  distribution and its values no longer hold.

[[Anthropic]] publishes research on [[Constitutional AI]] and [[RLHF]] as
partial mitigations. [[OpenAI]] maintains the [[superalignment]] team.
Academic work at [[MIRI]], [[ARC]], [[Redwood Research]].

The [[scaling hypothesis]] underlies the urgency: if capability keeps
tracking compute, the next 10× will arrive faster than safety research
can keep up.
